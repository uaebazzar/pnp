Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ourzap ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our app and services.

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect the minimum amount of personal information necessary to provide and improve our services. Specifically, we collect only:

  • Your name
  • Your email address

We do not collect any other personal information, usage data, device information, or tracking data beyond these two essential items.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for specific, limited purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To communicate with you about our services and updates
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • To send you technical notices and support messages
  • To process any account-related requests

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We do not share user info with others under any circumstances.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your name and email address against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is stored using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Your Rights

Regarding the personal information we hold (your name and email address), you have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to processing
  • Data portability (receiving a copy of your data)

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
